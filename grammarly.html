📖 What is Grammarly AI?

Grammarly AI is one of the world's most popular AI writing assistants. It helps users improve grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity and writing style. It also provides AI-powered suggestions for emails, essays, articles and professional documents.

✨ Key Features

  • ✅ AI Grammar Checker
  • ✅ Spelling Correction
  • ✅ AI Rewrite Suggestions
  • ✅ Tone Detection
  • ✅ Plagiarism Checker (Premium)
  • ✅ Works with Chrome, Word & Google Docs

👍 Pros

  • Very easy to use
  • Free version available
  • Excellent grammar correction
  • Supports multiple writing styles
  • Trusted by millions of users

👎 Cons

  • Premium plan is required for advanced features.
  • Plagiarism checker is not free.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Grammarly AI free?

Yes. Grammarly offers a free version with grammar and spelling corrections. Premium plans unlock advanced AI writing features.

Can Grammarly AI write essays?

Yes. Grammarly AI can help improve essays, rewrite sentences, and enhance clarity, but you should always review AI-generated content before submitting it.

Is Grammarly better than ChatGPT?

Both tools are useful. Grammarly focuses on writing improvement, while ChatGPT is better for brainstorming, explanations, and content generation.
